Payroll Management

Our Payroll Management services are designed to simplify the payroll process, ensuring accuracy and compliance while saving you time. We offer:

  • Payroll Processing: Efficient and accurate payroll processing on a weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly basis, ensuring employees are paid on time and according to their employment agreements. We ensure timely compliance and source deduction remittance to CRA for our clients so that they can focus on their business.
  • T4/T4A/T5 Filing:Comprehensive preparation and filing of T4, T4A, and T5 slips, ensuring compliance with Canadian tax regulations and timely submission to the Canada Revenue Agency.
  • WSIB: Expert assistance with Workplace Safety and Insurance Board (WSIB) requirements, helping you manage claims and ensure compliance with workplace safety regulations.
  • ROE Filing:Professional support in preparing and filing Records of Employment (ROE), ensuring accuracy and compliance for employees leaving your organization.
