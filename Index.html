Welcome to Rakesh Majithia CPA

At Rakesh Majithia CPA, we are committed to helping individuals, families, and businesses navigate the complexities of accounting and tax. As a full-service Chartered Professional Accounting (CPA) firm based in Canada, our mission is to provide exceptional services that drive success, growth, and peace of mind. With offices Mississauga and Oakville, we proudly serve clients throughout Canada.

We are your trusted financial partner, dedicated to helping you achieve financial clarity, grow your business, and secure your financial future. With decades of collective experience, we offer a full range of Assurance Services, Accounting & Bookkeeping, Personal Tax, Corporate Tax, Payroll Management and Advisory Services to individuals, families, and businesses across Canada.

Our smart approach
  • With years of expertise in tax and accounting, we deliver customized solutions that meet your unique needs.
  • Our dedicated, personalized approach ensures optimal results for both businesses and individuals.
  • We provide a comprehensive range of tax, accounting, and consulting services from our Mississauga and Oakville offices.
