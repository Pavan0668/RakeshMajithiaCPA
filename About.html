About Us

Rakesh Majithia CPA Professional Corporation (RMCPA) is a leading CPA firm with offices in Mississauga and Oakville, specializing in tax, accounting, and consulting services for both businesses and individuals.

We aim to redefine traditional market models by offering a fresh and innovative approach to help clients achieve their financial goals, whether personally or professionally. Our dedication to client success motivates us to provide exceptional service, along with customized, cost-effective solutions designed to meet the specific needs of entrepreneurial clients.

Our experienced team, consisting of knowledgeable partners and associates, is well-versed in Canadian taxation and accounting. We work closely with both business and individual clients to provide expert advice on tax, accounting, and auditing matters.

We serve a wide range of clients, including small and medium-sized businesses across Ontario, Canada, and internationally. In addition, we assist entrepreneurs, tax-exempt organizations, individual taxpayers, and foreign nationals.

Our Founder

Rakesh Majithia, CPA



Rakesh Majithia is the founder of Rakesh Majithia CPA Professional Corporation, bringing over 20 years of accounting experience with a specialization in owner-managed businesses, startups, professionals, and individuals. He has worked across a wide range of industries, including real estate, manufacturing, hospitality, and food services.

A graduate of Ryerson University (Toronto Metropolitan University), Rakesh earned his Chartered Accountant designation in 2004 after gaining valuable experience in the public accounting sector. He has expertise in tax, business advisory, and assurance services, regularly consulting on strategic planning, financial analysis, profit improvement, internal control procedures, and tax planning for owner-managers and high-net-worth individuals.

Outside of his professional endeavors, Rakesh is an active board member for several organizations and is dedicated to supporting various charitable initiatives, reflecting his commitment to community involvement.
